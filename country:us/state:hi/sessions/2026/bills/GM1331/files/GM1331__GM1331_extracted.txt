A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO TOBACCO ENFORCEMENT.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that Act 62, Session Laws
2 of Hawaii 2023, established a new excise tax on electronic
3 smoking devices and e-liquids, requiring updates to tobacco
4 enforcement laws to ensure the State's enforcement authority
5 extends to these products.
6 The purpose of this Act is to update tobacco enforcement
7 laws to:
8 (1) Clarify that untaxed tobacco products are subject to
9 forfeiture as contraband, without regard to the
10 procedures set forth in chapter 712A, Hawaii Revised
11 Statutes, in the same manner as untaxed cigarettes;
12 and
13 (2) Confirm the authority of the department of taxation
14 and the department of the attorney general to inspect
15 and seize tobacco products, including electronic
16 smoking devices and e-liquids.
