A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO EDUCATION.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. (a) The department of education shall
2 establish a two-year student-authored book publication pilot
3 program in which students research, write, and publish original
4 works. The pilot program shall begin in the 2026-2027 school
5 year and be completed no later than the conclusion of the
6 2027-2028 school year. The pilot program shall be designed to
7 strengthen literacy, analytical thinking, and communication
8 skills; increase student engagement and confidence; support
9 students' academic progression and preparation for postsecondary
10 education; and foster connections to culture and community.
11 (b) The student-authored book publication pilot program
12 may be conducted at up to five Kalihi public elementary schools
13 and up to five Nanakuli-Waianae public elementary schools on an
14 opt-in basis and made available to students in grades four,
15 five, and six; provided that the pilot program shall be
16 integrated into the existing grade level curricula of
17 participating schools as a capstone.
