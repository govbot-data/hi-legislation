A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO KUPUNA HOUSING.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that the state rent
2 supplement program for kupuna administered by the Hawaii public
3 housing authority has helped prevent some of the State's
4 vulnerable elderly residents from facing eviction and becoming
5 homeless. In total, the program has provided about three
6 hundred at-risk kupuna with monthly rental assistance of less
7 than $500 per month. In 2024, the legislature funded the
8 program with $1,000,000 and in 2025, the legislature extended
9 the program's sunset date from 2026 to 2028. The legislature
10 believes that the program should now be made permanent.
11 Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to make the state
12 rent supplement program for kupuna permanent.
