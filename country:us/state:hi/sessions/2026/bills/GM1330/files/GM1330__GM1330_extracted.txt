A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO POLITICAL FUNDRAISING BY EXECUTIVE BRANCH EMPLOYEES.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1, The legislature finds that public confidence in
2 the integrity of state government depends on the assurance that
3 decisions about state contracts are made free from political
4 influence. When high-level executive branch employees engage in
5 political fundraising, it creates a risk — or the appearance —
6 that governmental authority could be used to reward political
7 supporters rather than being exercised in the public's best
8 interest.
9 The legislature further finds that preventing "pay-to-play"
10 practices, or even the perception of such practices, is
11 essential to preserving the trust of the people of the State in
12 their government.
13 The legislature further finds that this Act is narrowly
14 tailored to apply only to a small and clearly defined group of
15 high-level executive branch employees, those nominated or
16 appointed by the governor to compensated positions subject to
17 senate confirmation, whose position has influence on procurement
