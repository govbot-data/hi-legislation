A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO HEALTH.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that an estimated
2 twenty-five thousand elderly individuals in Hawaii who require
3 in-home health care or support services and do not qualify for
4 existing programs, such as medicaid, cannot afford in-home care
5 services.
6 Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to establish a one-
7 year KupunAloha pilot program within the department of health to
8 provide in-home health care and support services for individuals
9 who do not qualify for existing government programs and cannot
10 otherwise afford these services.
11 SECTION 2. (a) There is established within the department
12 of health a one-year KupunAloha pilot program to provide in-home
13 health care and support services for eligible individuals who do
14 not otherwise qualify for government assistance for these
15 services.
16 (b) In implementing the pilot program, the department
17 shall create a plan of care for each eligible applicant. The
