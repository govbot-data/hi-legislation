A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO THE SUPPLEMENTAL NUTRITION ASSISTANCE PROGRAM.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. (a) The department of human services shall set
2 the minimum certification periods for supplemental nutrition
3 assistance program benefits households to twelve months;
4 provided that for households in which all adult members are
5 sixty years of age or older, the certification periods shall be
6 twenty-four months at a minimum.
7 (b) The department of human services shall participate in
8 the elderly simplified application project of the supplemental
9 nutrition assistance program to simplify eligibility
10 determinations for eligible households. Participation in the
11 elderly simplified application project shall include;
12 (1) Establishing recertification periods of at least
13 thirty-six months for households composed entirely of
14 adults sixty years of age or older or adults with
15 disabilities; and
16 (2) Waiving the recertification interview for eligible
17 households, to the extent allowed by federal law.
